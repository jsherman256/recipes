## Pizza Sauce

Serves 2-4  
5 minutes active time   

### FODMAP Notes

### Ingredients
* 1 oz tomato paste
* 1 oz water
* 7 g (1/2 Tbsp) garlic-infused olive oil
* 1/8 tsp kosher salt
* 1/8 tsp oregano
* 2 g (1/2 tsp) sugar
* Dash of ground black pepper

### Directions
1. Combine all ingredients and mix well. Adjust to taste
